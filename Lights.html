Lights

What we do?

As a light tech our job is to make the show look good. We use all sorts of different lights and colours to make people attracted to the show. We also allow the people on stage to be seen by using a simple wash of light. In shows like Big Night In and Out we often get a bunch of moving lights and blinders in to add some effects to the show. Setting up all the lights can take a while, but the result is usually worth it.
